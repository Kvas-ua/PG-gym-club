Why You Should Join

The club offers many opportunities you wouldn't
otherwise see working out solo. Can be beneficial
to any athlete at PG. Here are a few benefits:

  1. A scoop of pre on entrance
  2. Is a good place to learn and improve, teeming
    with experienced athletes who can offer advice
    in any sport or team you might want to join
  3. Offers a place for those who don't have a lot
    time after school to workout
  4. Will add to your school experience with events,
    competitions, and opportunities to win and be
    recognized
  5. Being a part of one of the most uplifting,
    rewarding, and positive communities at PG
